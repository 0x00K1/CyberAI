Welcome to CyberAI

CyberAI is a Discord bot that uses AI to interact with users. You can use it to chat, play games, and more!

Commands

Here are some of the commands you can use with CyberAI:

  • !help - Displays a list of available commands
  • !chat [message] - Starts a conversation with CyberAI
  • !play [game] - Plays a game with CyberAI
