Commands

Here are some of the commands you can use with CyberAI:

  • $help - Displays a list of available commands.
  • $INFO - Provide information about me, the Assistant, including my training data and capabilities!
  • $Reset - Delete the previous conversations with the me.
  • #[Comment] - For comments, I'll not grep the request.
